How to use

Type your KaTeX into the textbox.
Click on the output to copy it as an image.

Firefox

If you use Firefox, you need to change your configuration.
Go to "about:config", search for "clipboard" and set "dom.events.asyncClipboard.clipboardItem" to "true".
